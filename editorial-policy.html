Our editorial principles

Rezpawns.com exists to help players find genuinely enjoyable games across Steam, Xbox, and PlayStation without wading through clutter. We don't host or run any games ourselves — every pick links out to its real, official storefront. This Editorial Policy explains how we decide what makes it into the library, how we describe it, and how we keep that process independent from advertising and affiliate income.

How we evaluate games

Before a title is added to the library, it is reviewed against a consistent set of criteria:

  • Quality: Is this a genuinely well-made, well-reviewed title we'd recommend even if we weren't asked?
  • Clarity of play: Is the core gameplay loop, genre, and style easy to understand from a quick look at the store page and our write-up?
  • Replay value: Does it hold up beyond a single session?
  • Appropriateness: Is it suitable for the general audience (13+) Rezpawns is built for, with its official age rating and content descriptors clearly and accurately represented on our page?
  • Availability: Is it currently available and purchasable on Steam, Xbox, and/or PlayStation, with accurate platform information at the time of listing?

Titles that don't meet this bar are either revised before publishing or left out of the library entirely.

Genre tags and platform facts

Genre labels, style notes, and session-length estimates shown on each game's page reflect our team's research and, where possible, hands-on time with the game. We do not display star ratings or scores — we'd rather describe why a game is worth your time than reduce it to a number.

Independence from advertising & affiliate relationships

Rezpawns.com may generate revenue through advertising and affiliate partnerships, as described in our Affiliate Disclosure. These relationships have no influence over which games are featured or how they're described. We do not accept payment in exchange for a more favorable listing.

Corrections & updates

If we discover an error in a game listing or other content — or if a reader points one out — we correct it as soon as practical. Significant factual corrections may be noted on the relevant page where appropriate.

Player feedback

Player suggestions submitted through our Contact page are reviewed by our curation team and may directly influence which games we evaluate next. We can't guarantee every suggestion will be added, but every message is read.

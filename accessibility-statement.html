Accessibility Statement

Home/Accessibility Statement

Last updated: June 30, 2026

Our commitment

Rezpawns.com is committed to providing a website that is accessible to the widest possible audience, regardless of ability or technology. We believe everyone should be able to browse our game library, read our content, and get in touch with us without unnecessary barriers.

Standards we aim to meet

We design and build Rezpawns.com with reference to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA, as a practical benchmark. This includes ongoing attention to:

  • Semantic HTML structure and a logical heading hierarchy;
  • Sufficient color contrast between text and background;
  • Keyboard-accessible navigation, menus, filters, and the game detail dialog;
  • Descriptive alternative text for meaningful images and icons;
  • Visible focus states for interactive elements; and
  • Responsive layouts that work across screen sizes and zoom levels.

Known limitations

Accessibility is an ongoing effort rather than a one-time fix. Some third-party embeds, ad content, or future game integrations may not fully meet the same standard we hold for our own site. Where we become aware of such gaps, we work to address them or provide an accessible alternative.

Assistive technology compatibility

We aim for Rezpawns.com to work well with common assistive technologies, including screen readers and keyboard-only navigation. If you encounter a barrier using your preferred assistive technology, we want to know about it.

Feedback & reporting issues

If you experience any difficulty accessing content or functionality on Rezpawns.com, please let us know. We take accessibility feedback seriously and will make reasonable efforts to address reported issues in a timely manner.

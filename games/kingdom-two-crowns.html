About Kingdom Two Crowns

Kingdom Two Crowns (Raw Fury) is a minimalist side-scrolling strategy game about recruiting subjects, building defenses, and holding your kingdom against nightly monster raids. The pixel-art presentation is deceptively simple for how deep the systems get.

Runs can be played solo or in two-player co-op, and each playthrough plays out differently depending on how aggressively you expand.

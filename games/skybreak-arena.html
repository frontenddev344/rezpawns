About Risk of Rain 2

Risk of Rain 2 (Hopoo Games) is a 3D roguelike where you fight through procedurally generated stages, stacking items that combine in increasingly ridiculous ways as your run goes on. It scales with how long you survive, so early stages feel nothing like late ones.

It supports full co-op, which makes it a strong pick if you're looking for something to drop into with friends rather than play solo.

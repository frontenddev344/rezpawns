About Sonic Mania

Sonic Mania (Christian Whitehead, Headcannon, PagodaWest Games) revives the pixel-art, momentum-driven platforming of the original Genesis games with new zones built on top of fan-favorite classics.

It's built for speed and replayability rather than a single linear story, which keeps runs short and makes it easy to jump back in for a few more minutes.

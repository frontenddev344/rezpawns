About Vampire Survivors

Vampire Survivors (poncle) strips action-roguelikes down to their core loop: move to survive, pick up auto-firing weapons, and watch your build snowball as the screen fills with hundreds of enemies at once.

Runs are short, unlocks persist between them, and the simplicity is deceptive — the depth comes entirely from weapon and item synergies you discover over time.

About Tetris Effect: Connected

Tetris Effect: Connected transforms the timeless puzzle formula into a breathtaking audiovisual experience. Every block you place syncs with dynamic music, vibrant visual effects, and evolving environments, creating a surprisingly relaxing yet deeply engaging rhythm as you play.

While the core gameplay remains instantly familiar, every stage introduces its own atmosphere through changing soundtracks, colors, and visual themes that react to your actions. It's a fresh take on one of gaming's greatest classics without losing what made it so addictive in the first place.

Connected expands the experience with online and local multiplayer modes, allowing players to cooperate against AI-controlled bosses or compete in classic versus matches. Whether you're playing alone or with friends, every session feels polished, rewarding, and endlessly replayable.

Easy to learn but difficult to master, Tetris Effect: Connected offers one of the most relaxing and visually memorable puzzle experiences available on modern platforms.

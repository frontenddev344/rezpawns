About Into the Breach

Into the Breach is a turn-based strategy game where every decision matters. Humanity's last hope rests in a small squad of powerful mechs tasked with defending cities from massive alien creatures known as the Vek. Each battle takes place on a compact grid, turning every move into an important tactical choice.

Unlike many strategy games, enemy attacks are revealed before they happen. Instead of reacting to surprises, you'll carefully study the battlefield, predict every move, and find creative ways to protect civilians while defeating your enemies. Sometimes pushing an enemy into another attacker is just as effective as firing your weapons.

Every campaign is procedurally generated, offering different maps, objectives, enemies, and rewards. Unlockable mech squads, pilots, and equipment encourage experimentation, making each new run feel fresh while gradually expanding your strategic options.

Despite its simple presentation, Into the Breach delivers remarkable tactical depth. Short missions, meaningful choices, and satisfying "one more run" gameplay have made it one of the highest-rated indie strategy games ever released.

Why we picked it

Into the Breach proves that great strategy doesn't require massive maps or complicated systems. Every battle feels like solving a clever puzzle where smart planning always beats luck. Its perfectly balanced tactical gameplay, endless replayability, and satisfying decision-making make it an easy recommendation for anyone who enjoys thoughtful strategy games.

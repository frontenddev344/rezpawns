About Outer Wilds

Outer Wilds (Mobius Digital) is an exploration game about a tiny solar system caught in a time loop that resets every 22 minutes. There's no combat and no traditional progression — everything you learn stays in your head, not on a skill tree.

Piecing together what's happening across dozens of loops, as you explore planets that are themselves changing over time, is one of the most distinctive discovery loops in any recent game.

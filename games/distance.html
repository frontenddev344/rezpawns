About Distance

Distance isn't your typical racing game. Every course is packed with moving hazards, massive jumps, gravity-defying roads, and split-second decisions that keep you focused from start to finish. It's less about simply being fast and more about learning how to survive at high speed.

Your vehicle is built for more than driving. It can jump, rotate in mid-air, stick to walls and ceilings, and even glide across huge gaps. These mechanics turn every level into a creative obstacle course where precision matters just as much as speed.

Beyond the campaign, Distance offers time trials, online multiplayer, split-screen play, and a huge collection of community-created tracks through Steam Workshop. Whether you're chasing leaderboard times or just enjoying the futuristic atmosphere, there's always another challenge waiting.

With its glowing neon environments, electronic soundtrack, and satisfying movement system, Distance delivers an arcade racing experience that feels fresh even years after its release.

Why we picked it

Distance takes the familiar idea of racing and turns it into something far more creative. Every track feels like a high-speed obstacle course where quick reflexes, smart movement, and perfect timing matter just as much as raw speed. The sleek visuals, satisfying controls, and endless replayability make it a standout recommendation for anyone looking for an arcade racer with a unique twist.

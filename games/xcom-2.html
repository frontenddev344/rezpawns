About XCOM 2

XCOM 2 (Firaxis Games) puts you in charge of a guerrilla resistance fighting an alien occupation, managing a mobile base, research, and a roster of soldiers across turn-based tactical missions where every decision carries permanent weight.

Procedurally varied maps and permadeath give it real tension — losing a veteran soldier stings in a way few strategy games manage, and the strategic layer above the missions keeps every campaign feeling different.

About Trackmania

Trackmania is a fast-paced arcade racing game where every fraction of a second matters. Instead of battling opponents on the track, you're racing against the clock, refining every turn, jump, drift, and landing until you discover the perfect run.

What makes Trackmania stand out is its creative approach to racing. Tracks twist through loops, steep climbs, sharp corners, and gravity-defying jumps that reward precision just as much as speed. The controls are easy to learn, but mastering each course can take hours of practice.

New official campaigns are released regularly, giving players fresh challenges throughout the year. Beyond that, thousands of community-made tracks, online events, and competitive leaderboards ensure there's always something new to race.

Whether you're looking to casually improve your lap times or compete against players from around the world, Trackmania delivers one of the most rewarding time-attack racing experiences available today.

About Risk of Rain 2

Risk of Rain 2 is a fast-paced roguelike shooter where every run feels completely different. Stranded on a hostile alien world, you'll battle relentless waves of enemies while collecting powerful items that can dramatically transform your abilities and playstyle.

Every stage challenges you to balance speed and exploration. Spend too much time searching for loot, and the world grows increasingly dangerous. Push forward too quickly, and you may find yourself underpowered for the battles ahead. This constant risk-versus-reward decision makes every run exciting from beginning to end.

Choose from a diverse roster of survivors, each with their own unique weapons, abilities, and strategies. Whether you prefer long-range precision, agile melee combat, heavy firepower, or tactical support, every character offers a fresh way to experience the game.

Solo play is rewarding, but Risk of Rain 2 truly shines in online co-op, where friends can work together, combine powerful item builds, and take on enormous bosses in chaotic battles that become more intense the longer you survive.

Why we picked it

Risk of Rain 2 perfectly blends fast-paced combat with rewarding progression. Every item you collect has the potential to completely change your build, creating unpredictable runs that encourage experimentation and keep players coming back for "just one more game." Whether played solo or with friends, it's one of the standout roguelike shooters of the past decade.

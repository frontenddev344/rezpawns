Our Story

A library built for five-minute breaks

Rezpawns began as a side project among a small group of players who loved discovering great games but were tired of how cluttered "best of" lists had become. We wanted something that respected a player's time: fast to browse, honest about what's actually good, and clear about where to go play it.

What started as a short list shared among friends has grown into a curated library spanning six genres. Every addition still goes through the same filter we used for our very first pick — is this actually worth someone's time, and would we recommend it without being asked?

Today, Rezpawns is run as a small, focused team that treats the picks list like a living product, not a static page of links dumped once and forgotten.

What "Rezpawns" means to us

A respawn is a second chance — a fresh start with everything you've learned still intact. That's the feeling we want every visit to the site to give you.

No grinding, no pressure, no countdown timers. Just games worth your next five minutes.

Milestones

How the library grew

2023

The first list

A small group of players puts together a short list of favorite games as a weekend project — and realizes there's a real appetite for honest, ad-light recommendations.

2024

Rezpawns goes live

The site launches publicly with an opening set of picks across action, puzzle and arcade genres.

2025

Mobile-first redesign

The entire experience is rebuilt around touch play and smaller screens, alongside a refreshed curation process for new additions.

2026

20 picks and counting

The library spans 20 curated titles across 6 genres, all linked directly to Steam, Xbox, or PlayStation, with new picks added regularly.

Small team, short feedback loops — every pick gets a second pair of eyes before it's featured.

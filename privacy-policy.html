1. Overview

Rezpawns.com ("Rezpawns," "we," "us," or "our") operates a free, curated game discovery hub. We do not host, embed, or run any games ourselves — every game we feature links out to its official storefront on Steam, Xbox, or PlayStation. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you visit our site, how that information is used, and the choices available to you. By using Rezpawns.com, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Rezpawns is a static website with no user account system and no payment processing. We have deliberately kept the data we collect to a minimum, but we still want to be precise about what does happen behind the scenes.

2. Information we collect

We collect information in a few limited ways:

Information you provide directly

  • Contact form submissions: When you fill out the form on our Contact page, you provide your name, email address, subject and message. This information is used solely to respond to your inquiry.
  • Newsletter sign-up: If you subscribe using the email field in our footer, we collect your email address to send occasional updates about new games.

Information collected automatically

  • Technical data: Like most websites, our hosting and analytics tools may automatically log standard technical information such as browser type, device type, approximate location (derived from IP address), referring pages, and pages visited.
  • Cookies: We use a limited set of cookies for site functionality, analytics, and — where applicable — advertising. See our Cookie Policy for full detail.

3. How we use information

We use the information described above to:

  • Respond to messages submitted through our Contact page;
  • Send newsletter updates to subscribers who opted in, and allow them to unsubscribe at any time;
  • Understand how visitors use the site so we can improve performance, layout and game curation;
  • Detect, prevent and address technical issues or abuse; and
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations.

We do not sell personal information, and we do not use contact form or newsletter data for purposes unrelated to those described here.

4. Cookies & tracking technologies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device. Rezpawns may use cookies for essential site functions, to remember basic preferences, to measure site traffic through analytics tools, and — if advertising is enabled on a given page — to support relevant ad delivery through third-party ad networks such as Google AdSense. Our full Cookie Policy describes each category in detail and explains how to manage your preferences through your browser settings.

If we run advertising campaigns through Google Ads, we may also use Google Ads conversion tracking and/or remarketing tags. These allow us to measure how visitors interact with the Site after clicking one of our ads, and may allow us to show ads to past visitors of Rezpawns as they browse other sites in Google's advertising network. This is done using cookies and similar technologies and does not identify you personally. You can opt out of personalized advertising, including Google's use of advertising cookies, by visiting Google Ads Settings or optout.aboutads.info.

5. Third-party services

We may rely on trusted third-party services to operate Rezpawns, including web hosting providers, analytics platforms, and advertising or affiliate networks such as Google AdSense and Google Ads. These providers may collect information directly through their own cookies or scripts, subject to their own privacy policies. Where our site contains affiliate links or sponsored placements, the relevant third party may also receive information about your visit through that link — see our Affiliate Disclosure for more.

We do not control the privacy practices of third-party sites linked from Rezpawns, and we encourage you to review their policies independently.

6. Data retention

We retain contact form submissions only as long as needed to resolve your inquiry and maintain a reasonable support record, typically no longer than 24 months. Newsletter email addresses are retained until you unsubscribe. Aggregated, anonymized analytics data may be retained longer, as it is not tied to an identifiable individual.

7. Your rights & choices

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or request deletion of personal information we hold about you, and to object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details below. You can also:

  • Unsubscribe from our newsletter using the link in any email we send;
  • Manage cookie consent at any time via the "Cookie Preferences" link in the site footer, or clear/block cookies through your browser settings; and
  • Opt out of personalized advertising through your ad network's preference tools, where available.

Your rights under GDPR (EEA/UK visitors)

If you are located in the European Economic Area or the United Kingdom, you have the right to access, correct, delete, or export the personal data we hold about you, and to object to or restrict certain processing, including for advertising purposes. Where we rely on your consent (for example, for advertising cookies), you can withdraw that consent at any time via the "Cookie Preferences" link in the site footer. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at +1 (302) 295-4800. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

Your rights under CCPA/CPRA (California residents)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to know what personal information we collect, to request deletion of that information, and to opt out of the "sale" or "sharing" of personal information (including via advertising cookies) as those terms are defined under the CCPA/CPRA. To submit a request, contact us at +1 (302) 295-4800 or decline advertising cookies via the "Cookie Preferences" link in the site footer. We do not knowingly sell or share the personal information of California residents under 16 without affirmative authorization.

8. Children's privacy

Rezpawns is intended for a general audience aged 13 and older, not for young children. Featured games carry their own official age ratings (for example, ESRB "Teen") and may include violence, blood, or other mature content descriptors — we encourage parents and guardians to check each game's official rating before purchase. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can promptly remove it.

9. Data security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect the information we hold. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security of any data submitted to us.

In short: we collect only what's needed to respond to you and keep the site running — no accounts, no payment data, and no sale of personal information.

10. International visitors

Rezpawns is operated from the United States. If you access the site from outside the United States, your information may be processed and stored in the United States or other jurisdictions with data protection laws that differ from those in your home country.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, operational, or regulatory reasons. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of Rezpawns after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

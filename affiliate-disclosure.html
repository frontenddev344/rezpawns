Our commitment to transparency

Rezpawns.com is free to use, and keeping it that way sometimes means relying on advertising and affiliate partnerships to support hosting, development and curation costs. This page explains, in plain terms, how those relationships work and how they may appear on the Site.

What is an affiliate link?

An affiliate link is a link to a third-party product, service, or website that includes a tracking code. If you click that link and take a qualifying action — such as making a purchase or signing up for a service — we may earn a commission or referral fee. This comes at no additional cost to you; the price you pay is unaffected by whether you arrived through an affiliate link.

Where affiliate links may appear

Affiliate links, sponsored placements, or paid partnerships may appear in game listings, articles, banners, or other content across Rezpawns.com. Where reasonably practical, we identify sponsored or affiliate content near the relevant link or section. If you are ever unsure whether a particular link is an affiliate link, feel free to contact us and we'll clarify.

Advertising

In addition to affiliate links, Rezpawns.com may display third-party advertisements served through ad networks such as Google AdSense. These networks may use cookies and similar technologies to deliver and measure ads, as described in our Cookie Policy.

Editorial independence

Affiliate relationships and advertising never determine which games we feature or how we describe them. Our curation decisions are made independently of any compensation arrangement — see our Editorial Policy for more on how we evaluate and select content.

Compliance

This disclosure is provided in the interest of transparency and in line with general guidance from the U.S. Federal Trade Commission (FTC) regarding endorsements and affiliate marketing disclosures.

Some pages may contain affiliate links, advertisements, or sponsored content, for which we may receive compensation. This never affects the price you pay or the independence of our game curation.

1. What are cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember information about your visit, such as preferences or browsing activity, which can make your next visit easier and the site more useful to you.

2. How we use cookies

Rezpawns.com uses cookies to keep the site functioning properly, to understand how visitors use the library, and — on pages where advertising or affiliate content appears — to support relevant ad delivery and measure performance. We aim to use as few cookies as the site genuinely needs.

3. Types of cookies we use

Essential cookies

These cookies are necessary for core site functionality, such as remembering your cookie preference or maintaining basic site security. The site may not function correctly without them.

Performance & analytics cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with Rezpawns.com — which pages are popular, how long sessions last, and where visitors run into friction — so we can improve the library and the overall experience.

Advertising cookies

Where ads are displayed, advertising partners (such as Google AdSense) may use cookies to deliver more relevant ads and to measure ad performance. These cookies may track activity across different websites you visit.

Affiliate tracking cookies

If you follow an affiliate link from Rezpawns.com, the destination site may place a cookie that allows us to receive credit for referring you, consistent with our Affiliate Disclosure.

4. Third-party cookies

Some cookies on Rezpawns.com are set by third-party services we rely on, including analytics providers and advertising networks. These third parties are responsible for their own cookie practices, and we encourage you to review their respective privacy and cookie policies for more detail.

5. Managing your preferences

When you first visit Rezpawns.com, a cookie consent banner lets you choose "Accept All" or "Necessary Only." Non-essential cookies — including analytics and advertising cookies — are not set until you choose "Accept All." You can change your choice at any time using the button below, or the "Cookie Preferences" link in the site footer.

Most browsers also allow you to control cookies through their own settings, including blocking cookies entirely or deleting existing ones. Be aware that disabling certain cookies may affect how parts of the site function.

6. Changes to this policy

We may update this Cookie Policy periodically to reflect changes in the cookies we use or for legal and operational reasons. Updates will be reflected by a revised "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
